Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

QuitBet AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App" or "Service").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication via magic link
  • Account Information: Profile data you provide during registration and use of the App
  • Journal Entries: Mood tracking, thoughts, recovery progress, and personal reflections
  • Financial Data: Transaction tracking, spending records, and savings information you enter
  • Task Completions: Recovery tasks, goals, and progress tracking data
  • Chat Messages: Conversations with our AI support system
  • Trusted Contacts: Emergency contact information you choose to provide (optional)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App Usage Data: Features used, session duration, interaction patterns, and navigation paths
  • Technical Data: App version, IP address, network information, and error logs
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchase history, subscription status, and transaction records (processed securely through Apple App Store or Stripe)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location Data: We do not collect, use, or share your precise location
  • Health Data: We do not access or collect health data from HealthKit or other health services
  • Biometric Data: We do not collect fingerprint, face, or other biometric identifiers
  • Contacts: We do not access your device contacts without your explicit permission

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

  • Provide and maintain our App services
  • Authenticate your account and manage your session
  • Deliver personalized AI-powered support and guidance
  • Track your recovery progress and provide insights
  • Process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions
  • Send push notifications (with your consent) for reminders and updates

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Conduct research and analytics (in anonymized form)

Safety and Security

  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats
  • Protect user safety in crisis situations
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your information:

Authentication and Database

  • Supabase: We use Supabase for user authentication, data storage, and database services. Supabase processes your account information, journal entries, financial data, and chat messages. Supabase Privacy Policy

AI Services

  • DeepSeek API: We use DeepSeek API to power our AI chat feature. Chat messages are sent to DeepSeek for processing to generate responses. DeepSeek Privacy Policy

Payment Processing

  • Apple App Store: For iOS in-app purchases, payment information is processed by Apple. We receive transaction confirmations but not your payment details. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: For Android and web purchases, payment information is processed by Stripe. We receive transaction confirmations but not your full payment details. Stripe Privacy Policy

Hosting and Infrastructure

  • Vercel: Our backend API is hosted on Vercel. Vercel Privacy Policy

Analytics (Optional)

  • Analytics services may be used to understand app usage patterns. If enabled, this data is anonymized and aggregated.

Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data stored in our database is encrypted
  • Secure Storage: Authentication tokens and sensitive data are stored using secure storage mechanisms
  • Access Controls: Data access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review and update our security practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the App. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to comply with legal obligations or protect rights and safety.

Emergency Situations

In crisis situations where immediate help is needed, we may share information with emergency services or trusted contacts you have designated (if you have enabled this feature).

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of non-essential data collection and processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: privacy@quitbetai.com

We will respond to your request within 30 days. In some cases, we may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active and you are using the App
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion, most data is deleted within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Anonymized Data: We may retain anonymized, aggregated data indefinitely for analytics and service improvement

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

By using our App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

We may send push notifications to your device for recovery reminders, important updates, crisis support resources, and app updates.
